About Us

Reliable Wind Turbine Component Repair

With over 20 years of expertise, we specialize in repairing critical FRP components, including wind turbine rotor blades, nacelle covers, and nose cones. Our skilled technicians use advanced tools and infrastructure to enhance turbine performance and efficiency. We have a proven track record in blade services, ensuring reliable solutions for complex repairs that extend turbine lifespan and optimize wind power generation.

Our services cover rotor blade repairs for leading edge erosion, lightning damage, and lamination defects. We also specialize in nose cone and nacelle cover repairs, addressing fire damage and structural integrity issues. Equipped with industry-leading techniques, we provide high-quality, cost-effective repair solutions tailored to customer specifications, ensuring durability and maximum energy output

Our Vision

To be a global leader in wind turbine maintenance and repair, setting industry standards with innovative techniques, advanced technology, and a commitment to excellence in renewable energy solutions.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, cost-effective wind turbine repair solutions that enhance performance, extend lifespan, and maximize energy efficiency, contributing to a sustainable future in renewable energy..
